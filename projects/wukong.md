# Black Myth: Wukong

## 1. Myth Reconstructed: The Destined One
"Black Myth: Wukong" is a dark, cinematic reimagining of the classic *Journey to the West*. Players take on the role of the "Destined One," retracing the footsteps of the Great Sage through perilous landscapes to uncover the truth buried within the ruins of legends.

## 2. Combat Philosophy: Powers of the Sage
The gameplay focuses on recreating the supernatural abilities of Chinese mythology rather than traditional parry-heavy mechanics.
* **72 Transformations**: Absorb the souls of defeated elite foes to transform into their forms and utilize their unique skills.
* **Spells & Arts**: Manipulate the battlefield with Taoist arts like "Immobilize" and "Cloud Step".
* **Staff Mastery**: Fluidly switch between Smash, Pillar, and Thrust stances to adapt to any threat.
